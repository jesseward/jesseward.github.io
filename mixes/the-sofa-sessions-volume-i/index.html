The Sofa Sessions I

ABOUT.

The Sofa Sessions mix series focuses on the soulful side of electronic music. The series features styles ranging from Acid Jazz, Downtempo, Drum and Bass, Hip-Hop and Trip-Hop. Music with soul, music to lounge to.

Volume I - Nod your head, tap your feet.. Easy listening for a lazy sunny Sunday afternoon. A mix of downtempo and jazzy hip-hop, inspired by the Mushroom Jazz mix series.
