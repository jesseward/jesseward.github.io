The Sofa Sessions II

ABOUT.

The Sofa Sessions mix series focuses on the soulful side of electronic music. The series features styles ranging from Acid Jazz, Downtempo, Drum and Bass, Hip-Hop and Trip-Hop. Music with soul, music to lounge to.

Volume II features classic Drum & Bass releases that helped define or were influenced by the Jazzy, Atmospheric and Intelligent Drum & Bass sounds that dominated the latter part of the 1990s.

Featuring releases from Moving Shadow, Good Looking Records, Earth and others.
